Welcome to our Project

This project is an electronic Go-kart!

Team Members: Adam Smrekar, Brandon Lewien, Jean-Christophe Owens, Arash Yousefzadeh

The project utilizes the concepts of embedded systems. It includes a master MSP and slave MSP via connected through bluetooth, one giving the instructions for direction and the slave sending the receiving data to the speed controller and outputting the chirp to the two motors. There was a source of power using 12 Volts. Look at link below to find code.
